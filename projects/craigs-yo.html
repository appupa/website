Craigs Yo

Get Yo'd for craigslist listings.

Release Date: 08/08/2014

Client: Yo

Website: craigsyo.co

Craigs Yo is a subscription gateway integrating Yo with Craigslist. The service sends a 'Yo' when your Craigslist search has a new listing, which triggers a push notification with a link to the search.
