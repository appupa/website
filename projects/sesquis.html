Sesquis

Find local solutions to your needs.

Release Date: 10/06/2014

Client: Sesquis

Website: sesquis.com

Sesquis operates a booking system built on a community platform, in geographical proximity to key Universities and Colleges on a global scale, connecting local demand for educational tutoring and assorted jobs with students -Solvers- in higher education. Appupa helped Sesquis with their web-based booking platform, written in Angular and Node.JS.
