Hack DFW

Dallas's first major student hackathon

Release Date: 10/06/2014

Client: HackDFW

Website: hackdfw.com

HackDFW is Dallas's first major student hackathon, and we created its website. Hosted by students at UTD, SMU, and UNT, HackDFW brings all of the Dallas area schools together in one exciting 24-hour event.
