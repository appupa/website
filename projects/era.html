Release Date: 10/06/2014

Client: ERA

Website: era.com

ERA Real Estate was founded on the premise of building a franchise system based on the principle of collaboration- the idea that by working together and helping one another, a stronger community of real estate professionals could be built. Each ERA office helps strengthen the communities where they work and practice real estate.

We developed WeSkill for ERA, a platform for training employees. To build it, we used PHP, MySQL, JavaScript, and HTML/CSS.
